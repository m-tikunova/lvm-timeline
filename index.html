8/6/2025

First rain. Possible mid air transfer for F4.

First rain for the juveniles. I found F4 early on and followed her for most of the time. She received prey (possibly via a midair transfer) and ate it on a roof near the S. Atlantic dead end, with Mom watching over her. Saw Dad flying around between 7:30am and 8:00am. At 9:10am, I saw all three juveniles in the trees at the S. Massachusetts dead end. At 9:25am, I left F4, now all wet from the rain, in the madrone tree by Colman Park, where I first found her.
